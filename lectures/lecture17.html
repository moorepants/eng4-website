Section Views

  • Sections are used to clarify interior construction that cannot be clearly described by hidden lines in exterior views.
  • Section views are obtained by an imaginary cutting plane passed through the object perpendicular to the line of sight.
  • The portion of the object between the cutting plane and the observer is assumed to be removed and section lines indicate the exposed surfaces.
  • Hidden lines behind the cutting plane are omitted in section views.
  • The cut surfaces are indicated using cross hatching
